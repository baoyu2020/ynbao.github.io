Biography

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at City University of Hong Kong, working with Prof. Kede Ma. My research interests include Visual Data Compression and AI System Security and Deployment.

I received my Ph.D. degree in Information and Communication Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2024, supervised by Prof. Yongsheng Liang. Prior to that, I obtained my M.E. degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2019 and B.E. degree from Harbin Engineering University in 2016.

I also have industry experience as an Intelligent System Engineer at Mindray and as a Research Intern at Peng Cheng Laboratory.
